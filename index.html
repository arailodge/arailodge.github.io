About Arai Lodge

Located 15 minutes from Lotte Arai ski resort and 15 minutes from Myoko Akakura, the "lodge" is an old house in serious need of new plumbing and electrical work. It likely also requires some heating and insulation. The Jōetsumyōkō Shinkansen Station is 20 minutes drive North.

Purchase was completed in late 2021 purely via email communication with the real-estate agent in Niigata.

Restoration to begin in mid 2022. Come back for updates.
